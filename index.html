About me

I am a postdoc at Lehrstuhl D für Mathematik of RWTH Aachen, hosted by Prof. Gabriele Nebe. (I was also here from 2014 to 2015.)

From 2015 to 2017, I was a postdoc at Department of EE – Systems of Tel Aviv University, working with Dr. Ofer Shayevitz.

From 2009 to 2014, I was a PhD student at Department of Mathematics of Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. Gennian Ge.

My research interests are combinatorics, information theory, lattices and coding theory.
