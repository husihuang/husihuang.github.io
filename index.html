About me

I am a postdoc at Department of Electrical Engineering – Systems of Tel Aviv University, working with Dr. Ofer Shayevitz.

From 2014 to 2015, I was a postdoc at Lehrstuhl D für Mathematik of RWTH Aachen, hosted by Prof. Gabriele Nebe.

Before that, I was a PhD student at Department of Mathematics of Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. Gennian Ge.

My research interests are combinatorics, information theory, lattices and coding theory.
